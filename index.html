Musubi Recipes

Classic Spam Musubi

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of Spam
  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked and seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets, cut into strips
  • Soy sauce for brushing

Instructions:

  1. Slice the Spam into 1/4-inch thick pieces and pan-fry until lightly crispy.
  2. Place the musubi press on a clean surface, lay a strip of nori across it, and add a layer of rice.
  3. Top the rice with a slice of fried Spam.
  4. Add another layer of rice on top of the Spam and press down with the musubi press to compact it.
  5. Fold the nori strip over the top and use a little water or soy sauce to seal the edge.
  6. Remove from the press and set aside. Repeat for more musubi.

Teriyaki Chicken Musubi

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked and seasoned
  • Nori sheets, cut into strips

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken thighs in teriyaki sauce for at least 30 minutes, then grill or pan-fry until cooked through.
  2. Slice the cooked chicken into thin strips.
  3. Follow the same musubi assembly process as described in the previous recipe, using the teriyaki chicken instead of Spam.

Vegetarian Avocado Musubi

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 2 cups sushi rice, cooked and seasoned
  • Nori sheets, cut into strips
  • Sesame seeds (optional)
  • Soy sauce for dipping

Instructions:

  1. Lay a strip of nori on the musubi press and add a layer of seasoned rice.
  2. Place avocado slices on top of the rice.
  3. Add another layer of rice and press down with the musubi press.
  4. Fold the nori strip over the top to seal the musubi.
  5. Optionally, sprinkle sesame seeds on top for added flavor and texture.
  6. Serve with soy sauce for dipping.
